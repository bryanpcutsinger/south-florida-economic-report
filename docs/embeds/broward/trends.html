Employment & Salary Trends

Between 2019 and 2025, employment in Broward went from 820,616 to 870,168, an increase of 6.0%. Average annual wages went from $56,732 to $79,664, rising 40.4% over the same period.

Source: BLS QCEW — Quarterly
