Employment & Salary Trends

Between 2019 and 2025, employment in Palm Beach went from 620,166 to 682,552, an increase of 10.1%. Average annual wages went from $58,240 to $88,920, rising 52.7% over the same period.

Source: BLS QCEW — Quarterly
